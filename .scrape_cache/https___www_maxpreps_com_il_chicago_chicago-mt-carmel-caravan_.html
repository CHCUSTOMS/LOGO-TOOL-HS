About Chicago Mt. Carmel

Mount Carmel High School, home of the Caravan, is a private high school located in Chicago, Illinois. Mount Carmel High School is part of the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Chicago and has approximately 1250 students and 17 varsity sports. Mount Carmel High School is dedicated to providing their students with an outstanding education in an all-male environment in which they can thrive intellectually.
