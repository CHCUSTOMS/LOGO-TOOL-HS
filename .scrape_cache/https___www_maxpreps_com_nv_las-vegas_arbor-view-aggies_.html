About Arbor View

Arbor View High School in Las Vegas, NV is an educational institution known for its exceptional athletics programs. With a strong focus on promoting physical activity and teamwork, Arbor View offers a wide range of opportunities for students to engage in sports and compete at both local and state levels. The school takes great pride in its athletics programs and strives to foster a sense of discipline, dedication, and sportsmanship among its students. Arbor View High School is an active participant in the state athletics association, which ensures that its athletics programs adhere to established standards and regulations. The school's association with this governing body allows it to provide students with a wide array of competitive opportunities, allowing them to showcase their skills and talents. By participating in various sports, students have the chance to not only improve their physical fitness but also develop essential life skills such as leadership, teamwork, and resilience. Arbor View High School's commitment to excellence in athletics provides a supportive environment that encourages students to reach their full potential both on and off the field.
