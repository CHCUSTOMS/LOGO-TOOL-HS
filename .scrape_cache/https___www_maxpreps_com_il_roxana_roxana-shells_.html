About Roxana

Roxana High School, located in the town of Roxana, IL, is a shining example of excellence in education and athletics. With a strong focus on academic achievement and holistic development, Roxana High School provides its students with a supportive and enriching environment. The school's athletics programs have gained recognition and admiration from students, parents, and the community. Known for their dedication, discipline, and sportsmanship, Roxana's student-athletes consistently showcase their talents in a variety of sports. The school is a proud member of the state athletics association, ensuring that their athletic programs adhere to high standards and compete at a competitive level against other schools in Illinois. Roxana High School's athletic teams, from basketball and football to volleyball and track and field, consistently display a relentless drive for success while fostering teamwork and camaraderie. Roxana High School is a testament to the power of education and athletics working in harmony. It prepares students for a bright future, both academically and athletically, while instilling values of discipline, teamwork, and perseverance. Roxana High School provides a nurturing environment where students can thrive and excel, both on the sports field and in their academic pursuits.
