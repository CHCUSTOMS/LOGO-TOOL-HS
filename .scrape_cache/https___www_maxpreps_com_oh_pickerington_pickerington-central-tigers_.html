About Pickerington Central

Pickerington Central High School, located in the community of Pickerington, Ohio, is a renowned educational institution known for its excellence in academics and athletics. With a strong emphasis on fostering holistic development, this school has created a positive and supportive environment for its students. At Pickerington Central High School, the athletics program is at the heart of the community's spirit. The school's dedication to sports is evident through its state-of-the-art facilities and passionate coaching staff. Whether it's football, basketball, soccer, or track and field, the athletes at Pickerington Central flourish under the guidance of talented trainers and experienced coaches. The school's commitment to athletic development is further bolstered by its affiliation with the Ohio High School Athletic Association, which ensures that student-athletes receive a well-rounded education and have opportunities to compete at the highest level. Pickerington Central High School fosters a sense of unity and camaraderie among its students and faculty, creating an environment for individual growth and community pride. With its outstanding athletics programs and affiliation with the Ohio High School Athletic Association, Pickerington Central High School truly stands out as an example of excellence and opportunity for young athletes in the Pickerington community.
