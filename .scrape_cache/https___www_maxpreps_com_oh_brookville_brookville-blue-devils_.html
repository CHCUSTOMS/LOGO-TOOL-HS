About Brookville

Situated in the town of Brookville, Ohio, Brookville High School is an educational institution known for its strong sense of community and dedication to student success. With a rich history and a commitment to fostering well-rounded individuals, Brookville High offers a diverse range of opportunities for its students. One prominent aspect of the school's identity is its acclaimed athletics programs. Brookville High School boasts a wide variety of sports teams, providing students with the chance to excel both on and off the field. Whether it's football, basketball, soccer, or swimming, the school's dedicated coaches and passionate student-athletes consistently demonstrate a strong work ethic and a commitment to teamwork. The school takes great pride in its athletes, fostering a supportive and inclusive environment that encourages both individual growth and collective achievement. Brookville High School is a proud member of the Ohio High School Athletic Association (OHSAA), an organization that upholds the values of sportsmanship, fair play, and integrity in high school athletics across the state. As a member of this association, Brookville High School competes with other schools in the area, engaging in spirited competition while embodying the principles of good sportsmanship. The school's affiliation with OHSAA further emphasizes its commitment to providing students with opportunities to develop athletic skills, build character, and forge lifelong friendships.
