About Staunton

Staunton High School, located in the town of Staunton, IL, is a thriving educational institution known for its exceptional athletics programs. With a strong focus on promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship, Staunton High School offers a wide range of athletic opportunities that cater to diverse student interests and abilities. Whether students are passionate about football, basketball, soccer, baseball, or other sports, they can find a supportive and inclusive environment to pursue their athletic dreams. Staunton High School takes great pride in its affiliation with the Illinois High School Association (IHSA), the governing body for high school athletics in the state. Through this association, the school has been able to provide students with numerous opportunities to compete at regional, sectional, and state levels, allowing them to showcase their skills and dedication. The IHSA also ensures fair and equitable competition, placing a strong emphasis on promoting values such as fairness, sportsmanship, and respect among participating schools and athletes. Staunton High School's active involvement in the IHSA speaks volumes about its commitment to fostering a well-rounded student experience that extends beyond the classroom.
