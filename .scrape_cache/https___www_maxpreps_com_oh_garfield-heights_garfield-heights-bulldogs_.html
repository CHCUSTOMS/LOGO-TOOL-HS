About Garfield Heights

Garfield Heights High School, located in the community of Garfield Heights, OH, is an example of excellence in education and athletics. With a strong emphasis on fostering personal growth, this school provides a nurturing environment that encourages students to reach their full potential. Garfield Heights High School boasts an impressive athletics program that instills discipline, teamwork, and perseverance in its student-athletes. From football to basketball, track and field to soccer, the school offers a wide range of sports activities that cater to every interest and skill level. The dedication of the coaching staff ensures that students receive top-notch training and support to excel in their chosen sports. As a member of the state athletics association, Garfield Heights High School holds itself to the highest standards of sportsmanship and fairness. The school instills values of integrity and respect in its athletes, encouraging them to compete with grace and honor. Through participation in various tournaments and competitions, students are able to showcase their talents and represent their school with pride. Garfield Heights High School's commitment to cultivating well-rounded individuals extends beyond the classroom, creating an environment where students can thrive both academically and athletically.
