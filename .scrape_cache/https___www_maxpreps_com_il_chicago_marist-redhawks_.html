About Marist

Marist High School, home of the Redhawks, is a private high school located in Chicago, Illinois. Marist High School is part of the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Chicago and has approximately 1750 students and 30 varsity sports. The mission of Marist High School as a Roman Catholic, coed high school, is to make Jesus Christ known and loved in the Marist Brothers' tradition, while preparing students for higher education and life.
