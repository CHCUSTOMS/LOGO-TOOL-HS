About Waukegan

Waukegan High School, located in the city of Waukegan, IL, is an example of excellence in education and athletic achievement. With a rich history and a deep commitment to fostering the potential of each student, Waukegan High School stands out as a premier institution. The school's athletics programs are nothing short of exceptional. Offering a wide range of sports, from football to basketball, soccer to track and field, Waukegan High School provides students with ample opportunities to develop their athletic skills and engage in healthy competition. The state athletics association, known for its robust support of high school athletics, plays a vital role in promoting fair play and ensuring a level playing field for all participants. Waukegan High School proudly participates in this association, further solidifying its commitment to excellence in sportsmanship, teamwork, and personal growth through athletics. Waukegan High School embodies the spirit of community and dedication to the holistic development of its students. With a thriving athletics program and a strong affiliation with the state athletics association, students at Waukegan High School are empowered to reach their full potential on and off the field, creating a positive and enriching high school experience.
