About St. Charles

St. Charles Preparatory School, located in the city of Columbus, OH, is an example of academic excellence and personal growth. With a rich history dating back to its founding in 1923, the school remains dedicated to providing a well-rounded education that nurtures the minds, hearts, and bodies of its students. The school's athletics programs are an integral part of the St. Charles experience, fostering teamwork, discipline, and a healthy competitive spirit. Its state athletics association membership ensures that students are provided with opportunities to compete against other top-tier schools in Ohio, while upholding the highest standards of sportsmanship and fair play. Whether it's on the football field, basketball court, or on the track, St. Charles student-athletes consistently demonstrate unwavering dedication and determination, showcasing their talents and bringing pride to their school community.
