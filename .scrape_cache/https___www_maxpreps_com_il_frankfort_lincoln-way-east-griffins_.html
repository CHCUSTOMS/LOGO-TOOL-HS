About Lincoln-Way East

Lincoln-Way East High School, located in the community of Frankfort, IL, is a renowned educational institution that consistently delivers excellence in both academics and athletics. With a strong commitment to developing well-rounded individuals, the school prides itself on fostering a supportive and inclusive environment for all students. Lincoln-Way East's athletics programs are second to none, providing students with exceptional opportunities to excel in a variety of sports. The school's state-of-the-art facilities, coupled with dedicated coaches and staff, enable students to develop both their physical skills and sportsmanship. Whether it's football, basketball, soccer, or track and field, Lincoln-Way East's athletes consistently demonstrate their competitive spirit and commitment to teamwork. Lincoln-Way East is an active member of the state athletics association, ensuring that their sports programs adhere to high standards of fairness and integrity. Through participation in state tournaments and competitions, students have the chance to showcase their abilities on a larger stage and build lifelong memories. Lincoln-Way East's dedication to promoting athletic excellence is evident in their students' achievements, which consistently place the school among the top contenders in various sports at the state level.
