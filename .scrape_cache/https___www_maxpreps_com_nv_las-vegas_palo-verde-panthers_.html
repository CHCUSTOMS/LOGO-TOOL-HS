About Palo Verde

Palo Verde High School, located in Las Vegas, NV, is a renowned educational institution that consistently fosters an environment of excellence and personal growth. With an impressive range of athletics programs, Palo Verde offers its students a diverse array of opportunities to explore their athletic talents and reach their full potential. The school's athletics programs are not only highly competitive but also emphasize the importance of sportsmanship and teamwork. Students have the chance to participate in a wide range of sports, including football, basketball, soccer, track and field, and many more. Palo Verde's commitment to athletic excellence is further demonstrated through its affiliation with the Nevada Interscholastic Activities Association, ensuring that all sporting events are conducted with integrity and fair play. With state-of-the-art facilities that cater to each sport's unique requirements, Palo Verde High School provides a supportive environment where students can thrive both academically and athletically.
