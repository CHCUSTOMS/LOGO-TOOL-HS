About Latin

The Latin School of Chicago, located in the city of Chicago, IL, is an educational institution known for its exceptional academic programs and strong emphasis on character development. With a rich heritage of over a century, the Latin School prides itself on fostering a supportive and inclusive learning environment that encourages students to reach their full potential. In addition to its exceptional academics, the Latin School of Chicago places great importance on its athletics programs. The school boasts a wide variety of sports teams that cater to diverse interests and skill levels. From competitive varsity teams to recreational intramural sports, the athletics programs at the Latin School promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship. With a focus on holistic development, these programs provide students with valuable opportunities to learn important life skills such as discipline, perseverance, and leadership. The Latin School of Chicago is a member of a respected state athletics association, which further enhances the quality of its athletics programs. This affiliation allows the school's athletes to compete at a high level and participate in a wide range of interscholastic competitions. Through dedicated coaching, state-of-the-art facilities, and a supportive community, the Latin School's athletics programs continue to excel, enriching the overall educational experience for its students.
