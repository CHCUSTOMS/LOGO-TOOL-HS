About Lemont

Lemont High School, located in Lemont, IL, is an educational institution that offers students an excellent academic and extracurricular experience. Known for its exceptional athletics programs, the school takes great pride in promoting physical fitness and teamwork among its students. With a wide range of sports offered, including basketball, baseball, soccer, volleyball, and more, Lemont High School provides ample opportunities for students to engage in competitive sports and showcase their talents. The school is affiliated with the Illinois High School Association (IHSA), which underscores its commitment to high standards and fair play in athletics. Through the IHSA, Lemont High School's sports teams have the opportunity to compete against other schools in the region, fostering healthy competition and camaraderie between students from different communities. The school's athletics programs also promote discipline, leadership, and a strong sense of community, instilling these values in students as they strive for excellence on and off the field. Lemont High School's robust athletics offerings, affiliation with the IHSA, and dedication to fostering teamwork and sportsmanship make it a standout choice for students seeking a well-rounded high school experience.
