About Firelands

Firelands High School, located in scenic Oberlin, OH, is an educational institution that fosters a nurturing and inclusive environment for its students. This school boasts an impressive athletics program that instills values of teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship among its young athletes. With an array of sports offered, from football to basketball, baseball to volleyball, students at Firelands High School have ample opportunities to pursue their athletic passions and showcase their talents. At Firelands High School, the passion for sports extends beyond the playing field. The school is proudly affiliated with the Ohio High School Athletic Association, ensuring that the athletics programs meet the highest standards and follow statewide guidelines. This affiliation not only enhances the competitiveness of Firelands' athletic teams but also provides students with an opportunity to participate in state-level competitions, where they can showcase their skills and compete against some of the finest athletes in Ohio. Firelands High School's dedication to fostering excellence in both academics and athletics creates a well-rounded educational experience for its students, preparing them for success in all aspects of life.
