About Pickerington North

Located in Pickerington, Ohio, Pickerington North High School is a thriving educational institution known for its exceptional commitment to academics and athletics. With a diverse student body and a dedicated faculty, the school offers a comprehensive curriculum that prepares students for success in college and beyond. Pickerington North High School boasts a robust athletics program that emphasizes teamwork, discipline, and a strong work ethic. The school is a member of the Ohio High School Athletic Association, participating in various sports at both the varsity and junior varsity levels. From football to basketball, soccer to swimming, students have the opportunity to showcase their talents and compete against other schools in the state. The school's athletes consistently demonstrate sportsmanship and dedication, making Pickerington North High School a force to be reckoned with in the realm of high school sports.
