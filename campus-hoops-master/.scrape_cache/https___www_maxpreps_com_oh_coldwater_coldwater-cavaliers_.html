About Coldwater

Coldwater High School, located in the welcoming town of Coldwater, OH, is an educational institution known for its exceptional dedication to fostering both academic and athletic excellence. With a proud history, this school stands tall as a pillar of the community, offering students a wide range of opportunities for personal growth and achievement. At Coldwater High School, the athletics programs take center stage, inspiring a sense of camaraderie and instilling valuable life skills in their student-athletes. The school is a proud member of the Ohio High School Athletic Association (OHSAA), participating in a variety of sports that cater to diverse interests and abilities. From fierce competition on the football field to thrilling moments on the basketball court, Coldwater High School athletes are consistently admired for their sportsmanship, dedication, and commitment to excellence. The school's state athletics association affiliation demonstrates its commitment to providing a comprehensive athletic program that prioritizes the well-rounded development of its students, helping them build confidence, teamwork, and a strong work ethic. Coldwater High School in Coldwater, OH, is an example of educational excellence that goes beyond the classroom, offering students a remarkable experience through its robust athletics programs and affiliation with the state athletics association. With a commitment to holistic development, this school empowers its students to reach their full potential both on and off the field, instilling values that will guide them towards a successful future.
