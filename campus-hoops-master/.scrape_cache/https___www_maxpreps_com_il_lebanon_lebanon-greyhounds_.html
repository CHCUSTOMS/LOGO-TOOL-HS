About Lebanon

Lebanon High School, located in Lebanon, IL, is an educational institution that offers a well-rounded experience for its students. With a strong emphasis on athletics, Lebanon High School provides numerous opportunities for students to participate in various sports and excel in their chosen fields. The school prides itself on its athletics programs, which foster teamwork, sportsmanship, and personal growth. Lebanon High School is a proud member of the state athletics association, ensuring that its athletic programs adhere to the highest standards and compete against other talented schools across the state. The school's commitment to fostering a holistic development extends beyond the classroom, as it provides state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated coaches who empower students to unlock their full potential. Lebanon High School is a place where students can thrive academically, socially, and athletically. Its athletics programs, supported by the state athletics association, create an environment where students can grow, compete, and achieve their goals. Lebanon High School's commitment to offering a comprehensive educational experience makes it an excellent choice for students seeking a well-rounded education.
