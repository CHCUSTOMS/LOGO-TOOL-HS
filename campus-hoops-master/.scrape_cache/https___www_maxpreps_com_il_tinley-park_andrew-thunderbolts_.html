About Andrew

Andrew High School, home of the Thunderbolts, is a public high school located in Tinley Park, Illinois. Andrew High School is part of the CHSD 230 and has approximately 2,100 students and 32 varsity sports. The mission of Consolidated High School District 230, a student-oriented center of learning, is to graduate young adults prepared to realize their life-long potential in an ever-changing world.
