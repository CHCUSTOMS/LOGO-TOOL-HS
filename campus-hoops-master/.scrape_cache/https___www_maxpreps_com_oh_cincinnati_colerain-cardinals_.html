About Colerain

Colerain High School, located in Cincinnati, OH, is a thriving educational institution known for its strong sense of community and dedication to excellence. The school boasts an impressive athletics program that has gained recognition both within the local community and at the state level. With a wide range of sports offered, Colerain High School provides students with ample opportunities to explore and excel in their athletic pursuits. Colerain High School is a proud member of the Ohio High School Athletic Association (OHSAA), which oversees interscholastic sports in the state. The school's affiliation with this organization ensures that their athletic programs uphold the highest standards of sportsmanship, fairness, and competition. The teams at Colerain High School consistently strive for greatness and are known for their hard work, determination, and team spirit. Colerain High School is an exceptional institution that emphasizes not only academic achievement but also the development of well-rounded individuals through its stellar athletics programs. By providing a platform for students to pursue their athletic passions, the school fosters a sense of camaraderie, sportsmanship, and personal growth within its student body.
