About Worthington Kilbourne

Worthington Kilbourne High School, located in the community of Worthington, OH, is renowned for its exceptional educational experience and thriving athletics programs. With a strong emphasis on both academic and athletic excellence, the school has fostered a culture of achievement and personal growth among its students. The athletics programs at Worthington Kilbourne High School are highly regarded, offering a wide range of competitive sports options for students to engage in. The school is a proud member of the Ohio High School Athletic Association (OHSAA), ensuring that its athletes have opportunities to compete at the state level. Whether it's football, basketball, soccer, or track and field, the school's athletics department instills a sense of discipline, teamwork, and sportsmanship in its student-athletes, preparing them not only for success on the field but also for their future endeavors. Worthington Kilbourne High School is an example of excellence in both academics and athletics. Its commitment to providing a well-rounded education, along with its athletics programs and affiliation with the Ohio High School Athletic Association, make it a top choice for students seeking a supportive and enriching high school experience.
