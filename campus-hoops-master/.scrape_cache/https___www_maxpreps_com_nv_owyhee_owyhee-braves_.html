About Owyhee

Owyhee High School, located in the town of Owyhee, NV, is an example of excellence in education and athletics. With a sprawling campus and a dedicated staff, this school offers an enriching and supportive environment for its students. Owyhee High School takes great pride in its athletics programs, which are renowned for their commitment to both individual growth and team spirit. The school's state-of-the-art facilities provide ample opportunities for students to engage in a wide range of sports, including basketball, football, track and field, and more. The school's athletics teams are affiliated with a respected state athletics association, ensuring that students have the opportunity to compete at the highest level, fostering a sense of resilience, discipline, and camaraderie among student-athletes. Owyhee High School is a symbol of achievement and community, offering a well-rounded educational experience along with a thriving athletics program. Through their unwavering dedication and support, the school instills in its students the values of teamwork, perseverance, and the pursuit of excellence.
